Who We Are

Welcome to JobsPic.com, your number one source for all things related to career hunting in Pakistan. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of daily job alerts, with a focus on authenticity, regular updates, and government sector transparency.

Founded in , JobsPic.com has come a long way from its beginnings. When we first started out, our passion for helping students and fresh graduates reduced the unemployment gap drove us to do intense research so that JobsPic.com can offer you the nation's most advanced career portal.

Our Mission

Our goal is straightforward: To bridge the gap between talented individuals and reputable organizations. Whether you are looking for jobs in FPSC, PPSC, Pakistan Army, Police, or top-tier multi-national companies, our platform ensures you get the notification first.

Why Choose Us?

  • Syllabus & Test Prep: Unlike generic boards, we provide context into the syllabus and interview preparation strategies.
  • Instant WhatsApp Alerts: Our massive WhatsApp community ensures that candidates never miss a deadline.
